Manual focusing at close range

Kurt Horsley (dpreview, Panasonic forum, 31 March 2004)...

At close range, you need to select ``off'' for the manual focus assist. That will prevent the 16X magnification ``box'' {that can bother you during manual focus}. It's too much magnification to use up close, but is very necessary at long distances.

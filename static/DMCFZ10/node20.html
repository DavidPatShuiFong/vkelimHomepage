Using the rear-panel LCD display

For those people who insist on using the rear-panel LCD display instead of the electronic viewfinder (EVF), Janis Whitcomb has a suggestion (Yahoo!, FZ10 forum, 26 March 2004)...

{A} way to stabilize a camera which has an LCD display is to put tension on the neck strap, holding the camera out tightly in front of you.

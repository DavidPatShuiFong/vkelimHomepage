User galleries

Many FZ10 users are posting their photographs onto the Internet. Some are posting their photographs onto the Panasonic forum of dpreview or Yahoo! (see ). Throughout this guide I have often `hyperlinked' a poster's name to their own photo gallery (if I know the poster's web-site). Here are a few more examples below...

Bill's (bjames) Lumix FZ10 gallery -- full of colourful (zoo) wildlife photography, flowers and some scenery.

Thomas Mussio's smugmug -- a wide variety of macro-animal life, New York streets and some moon shots.

SharonO's (sosberg) Antartica album -- almost makes me think of going to Antartica for a holiday! Note : you might need to register with ImageStation before accessing Imagestation albums.

Charlie Howard's FZ10 samples -- `test' photographs. Charlie Howard has numerous other albums of interest, including instructions on how to make your own manual white-balance lens cap (See ).

PBase FZ10 galleries -- I think this is a `composite' page of all the photographs taken with (and attributed to) an FZ10 on pbase.

Yahoo! FZ10 group -- Yahoo! FZ10 group `Picture of the Week' gallery. Weekly photography projects. Join in the fun!

Cokin creative ring flash

FZFun (Steve's Digicam, Panasonic forum, 24 December 2004)...

The ring flash is heavy, and I am still getting use to it. I am using the Cokin Creative Ring Flash. It should work fine on the FZ20. It is a rig that has three adjustable flashes on it. It has a big huge block that attaches to your hotshoe. There is a thick wire that extends to the bottom of the flash (more on this later). I had to get a P to A adapter (which also gives you three slots to use for Cokin filters...imagine that!), and then an adapter ring to get it to fit my camera...

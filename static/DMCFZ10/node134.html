Flippable/tiltable LCD display

If you have the time, patience and perhaps ability to read Chinese, you could add a flippable/tiltable LCD display to your FZ10. Sorry, I am unable to provide a translation for you. (I can't read Chinese either).

It looks like he has attached the FZ10's television output to an LCD display. The LCD display can either be attached to the flash hot-shoe or the tripod socket using a mounting bracket. The LCD display also requires power. The power source is most easily attached to the tripod socket.

Yahoo! Cityfish gallery -- Images of the tiltable LCD display attached to an FZ10. Original images and posting are in a DCView posting.

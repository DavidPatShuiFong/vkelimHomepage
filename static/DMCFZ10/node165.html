Contact and miscellany

Please send comments, suggestions and corrections to me at vkelim@bigpond.com. I also can be contacted via Steve's Digicam forums as `DavidFong' via private messaging.

Thanks to all the people who have made the Panasonic and FZ10 forums such a useful place to find information. Without them, this guide would obviously be impossible. Where possible, I will try to contact the poster of any messages I have quoted. If I have not correctly attributed the post, or you want me to correct your contact details or add a link to your photo-gallery, or even if you want a quotation withdrawn, please contact me. I have sometimes taken the liberty of directly linking to photographs available on photo-galleries, please inform me if you wish the link to be removed!

If I do not seem to respond to your request, please e-mail me again. I am now receiving so much `spam (junk e-mail)' it is becoming incredibly difficult to find out which e-mails are non-spam. Yes, I do use spam filters.

Where I have changed or modified a quote, I have placed my changes inside square brackets or curly braces. Unfortunately, sometimes (actually quite often) square brackets turns into numbers like `91' or `93'.

Note that I am not a camera/photography expert, and questions regarding operation and use of the DMC FZ10 are best directed to one of the forums (Section ). Similarly, these web-pages are merely a means of summarizing information already available on the web. In many cases, they provide information which are beyond my personal experience or ability to verify, and I can only advise you either to ask another question on the forums or seek confirmation and advice from other sources prior to using equipment/accessories/modifications/techniques which may endanger your camera or yourself (that's right! You just read a disclaimer!)

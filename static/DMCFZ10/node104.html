Pemaraal adapters

More information at Pemaraal website. PD62 (62 mm adapter). Test shots of the PD62 together with TCON-14B/17 teleconverters and WCON-07/08B wide-angle adapters. Unlike the Yoshida 55 mm adapter, the 62 mm adapter allows the Pansonic lens cap to be used.

As of June 2004, Pemaraal are planning to produce aluminium adapters for the FZ10 by July 2004. Thread sizes are expected to be available in 62mm, 58 mm and 55 mm. Some adapters will be `two-piece', incorporating a lens hood.

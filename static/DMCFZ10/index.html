Panasonic DMC FZ-10 rough guide

David P.S. Fong

Abstract:

The Panasonic DMC FZ-10 `Fluzi' is my first digital camera, and what a great choice it is! I come from a casual hobbyist SLR background (a trusty, and cheap, Sigma SA-300N), and found that the FZ10 was the first relatively inexpensive digital camera which could do roughly everything I expected a cheap SLR (single-lens-reflex) to do. In addition, it has reasonable macro capability, much better zoom than a typical SLR lens and optical stabilization. This guide/unofficial FAQ is gleaned from all the useful information I found on the Internet and Panasonic digital camera forums (Section ). A lot of the posters in the digital camera forums are much more experienced in digital and film photography than I could ever hope to be! I hope you find the information in this guide useful and enjoy taking great photographs with your FZ10.

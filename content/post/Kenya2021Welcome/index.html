Hello friends!

Welcome to the support, encouragement & prayer group for Rosalie and David. We are planning to start our journey on Dec 25th and to come back on Jan 7th.

Our aims for this trip are:

  • to encourage the workers and children in Kibera

  • giving out cards and gifts for the children.
  • training for the Spur workers / program evaluation / better communication
  • meeting and encouraging our new worker Andrew in rural Kenya (Holo, Kisumu)
  • going to schools in rural Kenya / visiting sponsored children & understanding their needs so that we can establish a new office in Holo, Kisumu
  • meeting with locals and establishing Spur Afrika in a new area - trying to start a Sunday program for children

We would love your prayers and thoughts. We have lots of paperwork and COVID related things to sort out before we go.
